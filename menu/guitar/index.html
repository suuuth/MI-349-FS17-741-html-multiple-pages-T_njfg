Guitars

YouTube taught me to play

I saw a friend of mine playing his guitar once in 7th grade, and ever since then I was hooked on the instrument. Sometime in 2007 I convinced my dad to help me buy my first electric and from then on I have taught myself everything. I enjoy listening to John Frusciante and Eric Clapton play, and I like Stratocaster-style guitars best for their flavorfully twangy sound.
